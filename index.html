Vad jag längtar dit

Have you heard the voice of the ice with it's powerful, crisp and crackling tone? Experienced Swedens largest all year round arcipelago? Watched elite basketball in one of the country's finest halls, or gazed upon the beautiful city's skyline from the skybar.. No? Then it's about time to experience Luleå

Destinations

Hot spots and beautiful views

Isbanan

At Isbanan you can go ice skating and kick sled. Take the opportunity to walk on the snowy sidewalk along the entire stretch. There are numerous barbeque spots and windshields postitioned out on the ice.

Luleå's City

The city center offers lots of experiences such as enjoyable restaurants, cafés, stores and night clubs. There's also a rich cultural life to discover such as Kulturens house, Norrbottens theatre, the city library, art galleries and museums.

Ormberget

In the winter you'll find well prepared skiing tracks, a biathlon plant and a slalom slope. During the summer Ormberget offers a running path and various biking tracks if mountainbiking is your thing.

NederLuleå Church

In Gammelstads kyrkby you can find Nederluleå kyrka, the assembly's main church. It's the largest medieval church in nothern Sweden.

Bälingeberget

The nature reserve Bälingeberget reaches 139 meters above sea level and forming fund towards älvdalens agricultural constructions. It's a accessible and very popular tourist site.

Arcipelago

The arcipelago of Luleå with it's 1312 islands is a beautiful and varying destination. Luleå's arcipelago is only a part of the larger Bottenviken's arcipelago which reaches from the city Skellefteå in the south to Haparanda at the border to Finland in northeast
